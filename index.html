White House Down

Plot

The film was released on June 28, 2013. The movie is about Capitol police officer John Cale (Channing Tatum) has just been denied his dream job of protecting President James Sawyer (Jamie Foxx) as a member of the Secret Service. Not wanting to disappoint his young daughter with the bad news, Cale takes her on a tour of the White House. While he and group attacks and seizes control. As the nation's government dissolves in chaos, only Cale can save his daughter, the president and the country.
